REST API
CRUD
Create: METHOD(POST) - Example /user or /users (req.body) StatusCode - 201 Created
Read: METHOD(GET) - Example /user/:id or /users/:id - StatusCode - 200 Ok
Update: METHOD(PUT or PATCH) - Example /user/:id or /users/:id (req.body or req.query or req.params) - 200 Ok or 204 No Content
Delete: METHOD(DELETE) - Example /user/:id or /users/:id (req.body or req.query or req.params) - 200 Ok or 204 or 202 deletion
